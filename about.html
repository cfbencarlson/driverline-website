About DriverLine

DriverLine was founded in 2025 to solve a real operational problem: auto parts stores and dealerships need reliable delivery drivers, but managing them in-house is expensive, complicated, and risky. We built a better way.

Our Mission

Remove the Burden of Driver Operations

Managing delivery drivers pulls focus away from what auto parts stores and dealerships do best: serving customers and moving inventory. The hiring, the compliance paperwork, the scheduling headaches, the liability exposure, the vehicle costs. It adds up fast.

DriverLine exists to take that entire burden off your plate. We provide vetted, insured drivers who show up ready to work, backed by systems designed for reliability. You get consistent delivery coverage with predictable costs. We handle the rest.

Our Approach

How We Think About Driver Operations

We built DriverLine around a few core beliefs about what delivery operations should look like.

1

Reliability Over Everything

When a driver doesn't show up, your customers notice. Our fleet lead system and backup coverage exist specifically to prevent coverage gaps. Problems get handled before they reach you.

2

Proper Compliance Matters

Independent contractor classification isn't optional. We use Gigsafe for IC management, run background and MVR checks, and ensure proper insurance coverage. The paperwork is done right.

3

Predictable Costs Beat Cheap Costs

Surprise expenses from vehicle breakdowns, accidents, or turnover hurt more than a slightly higher predictable rate. Our model removes those variables from your operation.

4

Flexibility Without Chaos

Your delivery needs change. New locations, seasonal volume, market shifts. We scale coverage up or down as needed without locking you into rigid contracts or leaving you understaffed.

What We Do

End-to-End Driver Operations

DriverLine handles the full scope of delivery driver operations for your locations.

Driver Sourcing & Vetting

We recruit, screen, and onboard qualified drivers. Background checks, MVR verification, and insurance requirements handled before any driver touches your routes.

Route Design & Setup

We evaluate your delivery patterns, design efficient routes, and handle onsite implementation. Routes built around your actual operation, not generic templates.

Compliance Management

IC classification through Gigsafe, required insurance verification, local compliance documentation. We maintain the paperwork so you don't have to.

Daily Operations

We manage day-to-day driver operations. Scheduling, issue resolution, performance monitoring, and communication. You focus on your business.

Backup Coverage

Fleet leads and contingency drivers handle callouts and failures. When something goes wrong with a driver, coverage continues without scrambling on your end.

Scalable Coverage

Add coverage at new locations or reduce at existing ones as your business needs change. Our model adapts to your operation, not the other way around.

Insurance & Risk

Proper Coverage. Reduced Exposure.

Delivery operations carry real risk. Our insurance structure and compliance approach protect both your business and ours.

Driver Insurance Requirements

Every DriverLine driver carries required occupational hazard insurance and commercial liability coverage. We verify coverage before assignment and monitor ongoing compliance.

DriverLine Corporate Coverage

DriverLine maintains Hired and Non-Owned Auto insurance coverage. This provides an additional layer of protection beyond individual driver policies.

Background & MVR Screening

All drivers undergo thorough background checks and motor vehicle record verification. Drivers with problematic histories don't make it onto your routes.

IC Compliance via Gigsafe

Proper independent contractor classification matters. We use Gigsafe for IC management, ensuring documentation and compliance are handled correctly from day one.
