Privacy Policy

Who We Are

DriverLine is a delivery driver solutions service operated by ATOR Enterprises Inc. We provide outsourced, compliant delivery drivers to automotive parts stores and dealerships.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business name when you request coverage or contact us.
  • Business Information: Details about your delivery needs, locations, and operational requirements.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited and time spent.
  • Communication Records: Records of emails, calls, and messages exchanged with our team.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Evaluate your delivery coverage needs and provide service quotes
  • Communicate with you about our services and updates
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist with our operations (such as email platforms or CRM systems)
  • Professional advisors (legal, accounting) as needed
  • Government authorities when required by law

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may request to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@yourdriverline.com.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

SMS Terms & Conditions

About Our SMS Program

DriverLine, operated by ATOR Enterprises Inc., may send SMS messages to communicate with customers and prospective customers about our delivery driver solutions.

Consent

By providing your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive SMS messages from DriverLine. Message frequency varies based on your interactions and service needs. You are not required to consent to SMS messaging as a condition of purchasing any goods or services.

Message Types

You may receive messages about:

  • Responses to your inquiries
  • Service updates and scheduling
  • Coverage availability information
  • General business communications

Opting Out

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. After opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further texts will be sent unless you opt back in.

Help

For assistance, reply HELP to any message, call us at (315) 505-2365, or email info@yourdriverline.com.

Costs

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. DriverLine does not charge for SMS messages, but your carrier may.

Carriers

SMS services are supported on major U.S. carriers. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your phone number and opt-in data will not be shared with third parties for marketing purposes. See our Privacy Policy above for full details on how we handle your information.
